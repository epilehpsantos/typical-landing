Gmail integration

How Typical uses Gmail

When you choose to connect Gmail, Typical requests a single scope — gmail.send — to send follow-up emails on your behalf. It does this and nothing else:

  • Sends only emails you draft, review, and explicitly confirm in the app.
  • Cannot read, modify, or delete anything in your inbox.
  • Tokens are stored encrypted and never shared with third parties.
  • Disconnect any time from Account Settings — this revokes access immediately.

Typical's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
